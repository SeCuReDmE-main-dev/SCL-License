# Agent Instructions For SEL/SECL

- Preserve SEL-2.0 for general education projects.
- Preserve SECL-2.0 for educational cybersecurity, fraud-awareness,
  safety, and abuse-prevention projects.
- Use `LicenseRef-*` only as local metadata references when tooling needs a
  custom license key and the full license text is present.
- Do not claim OSI approval or SPDX listing unless it has actually happened.
- Do not remove ORCID attribution:
  `https://orcid.org/0009-0007-2904-0443`.
- Do not weaken the no-warranty, no-misuse-responsibility, or human-review
  boundaries.
- Do not add attack, theft, fraud, bypass, unsafe surveillance, or criminal
  automation workflows.
- Keep official school AI wording aligned with Codex/OpenAI and
  Antigravity/Gemini only.
