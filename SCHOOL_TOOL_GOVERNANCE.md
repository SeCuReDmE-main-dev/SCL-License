# School Tool Governance

The SecuredMe license family is for school-safe, review-oriented tools.

- `SEL-2.0` is for general educational software and research scaffolds.
- `SECL-2.0` is for educational cybersecurity, fraud-awareness, safety, and
  abuse-prevention tools.

Official classroom AI-assisted routes across the SecuredMe school suite are
Codex/OpenAI and Antigravity/Gemini only. Unsupported routes include Ollama
Cloud, generic uncensored local AI, and unknown agents.

Forks are allowed according to the selected license text. The official supported
school version is the reviewed maintainer version.
